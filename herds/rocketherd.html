About

The foothills herd consists of my earliest WMEs, and was the first herd I created for my equids. Their ranks are largely built on interlopers and loners, who've come together to form a family.

Terrain

Location Reference ⋆ Montana Valley Foothills
The foothills herd lives in a temperate grassland at the base of a large mountain range. The terrain is grassy and hilly, with some rocky outcroppings and small stands of deciduous trees. Summers a dry and warm, while winters are mild thanks to the cradle of the hills and valley.

Herd Dynamics

Choose two WMEs to read about their relationship!
Rocket and Soliloquy have a quiet, devoted relationship. They bring out the best in each other, with Rocket's brash personality being tempered by her quiet nature, and his earnest support aiding in her confidence. They can be quite cheeky with one another, a side of Soliloquy seen with nobody else.
Honolulu and Rocket are good friends, despite their frequent arguments. Honolulu can be very stubborn in her opinions (even when she's been proven wrong), but at the end of the day, Rocket does appreciate her experience and advice. Sometimes their frequencies align and they decide to be menaces together, largely when it comes to interacting with rival herds.
Ladybird looks up to Rocket, seeing him much like an older brother. He loves to indulge her mischevious streak, while also ensuring that her shenanigans don't lead to any injuries.
Rocket and Bruno have a rather neutral relationship -- Rocket respects Bruno's levelheaded calm, and Bruno respects Rocket's tenacity; however, the two don't spend much time together.
Mocha is Rocket's go-to for a little goofing around - her free spirited nature makes her an excellent playmate. She can get in his way though, sometimes inserting herself into an otherwise carefully planned bit of hijinks and causing things to go awry.
Classical has a bit of disdain for Rocket's childish nature, and believes he has a lot of growing up to do still. However, his heart is in the right place, and she's content enough to rest under his watchful eye. Rocket things she needs to mellow out a little bit.
Two bros spotting the shapes in clouds together. Zach makes an excellent sound board for ideas, and intimidating backup when Rocket needs to look fierce.
Tia looks up to Rocket as a leader, hoping that she will one day have his level of self-assuredness.
Renee thinks Rocket is a tiny little (definitely not the same height as her or anything) idiot with WAY too much confidence -- but she sticks around anyway. He tolerates her temper rather well, and doesn't hold it against her.
Rocket and Ciel mutually respect how genuine the other is - both openly speak their minds, even if they go about it a bit differently.
