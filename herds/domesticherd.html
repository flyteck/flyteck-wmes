Terrain

Location Reference ⋆ Ponderosa Pine Forest, Arizona
The boys spend their days meandering through sparse ponderosa pines and high yellow grass, foraging for roots and new sprouts. The climate is warm, with breezy winters and hot, dry summers. The short trees always let the sun and open sky through, although they do offer some shelter from the elements.
