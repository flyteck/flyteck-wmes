About

The Ocean's Embrace herd functions largely as a polycule - Caspian is the leader, and romantically engaged with all members, some of which are engaged with one another as well.

Terrain

Location Reference ⋆ San Josef Bay, Vancouver
The Ocean's Embrace herd lives along a cool, temperate, white sand beach; littered with rocky outcroppings and evergreen trees. The area is often overcast and gusty. The herd survives on small outcroppings of tough grass and lichen, as well as seaweed that washes up on the beaches.
