General

Overview

Tia is a lover of life. She loves to explore and learn, taking in the wonder of the world. She isn't particularly bright, and can be over trusting and naive. She can always find the positives in life and learns something new every day. She likes almost everyone she meets, and loves them for their differences. She's always in a good mood, and it's hard to be unhappy around her. She tends to be a bit over-happy sometimes, but can be calm and helpful if someone needs to talk.
