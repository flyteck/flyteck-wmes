General

Overview

Cain is high-strung and haughty, and he often always has someone doing work for him whether he had to manipulate them into doing it or throw a temper tantrum to get whatever he wanted. He doesn't like spending an entire day around people and will get very grumpy and make it clear when he's had enough. Normally, he only likes to spend a few hour with friends and strangers, and then he's done for the day. If a disaster was going to happen, Cain would not hesitate to save himself first over his friends. However, as soon as he falls in love with you he'll change everything around. He isn't loyal friend and family wise, but catch his heart and he'll be wrapped around your finger. (by kortico)
