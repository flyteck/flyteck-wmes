General

Overview

Honolulu is a very wise equid. She has several years under her belt, and knows all the tricks of both the mountains and humans. She is very maternal, acting as a second mother to Ladybird, and really, any foals she can find. This can sometimes irritate their actual parents, since she often comes out of nowhere and starts fawning over the foals (she will also sometimes complain about their parenting skills). She has a love for adventure, but doesn't go looking for trouble. She doesn't tend to plan these things out, but she is quick on her feet, and doesn't normally get into any real trouble. She's sarcastic and she knows it; one of her favourite things is messing with other equids, especially if they get annoyed. Despite this, she's very hypocritical and expects them not to do the same to her. Hana is often very condescending, but she doesn't mean it to be hurtful most of the time. She does have manners and can be polite and collected when it's required, but when it's not, she just likes to have fun.
