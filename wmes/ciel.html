General

Overview

Ciel is shy and timid. He constantly worries that others won't like him, or that he'll hurt someones feelings somehow. If spoken to, he'll answer honestly while considering the other equid's feelings. He likes company, although he doesn't talk too much. Ciel has a strong sense of justice, and he'll stick up for a complete stranger if he feels they need the help. Most other equids like him, although very few really understand him. Ciel, however, is very good at reading other equids, and basically understands all of them upon first meeting them, although he never really trusts his gut (even though it's right 98% of the time). He's very easy to fluster.
