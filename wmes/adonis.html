General

Overview

Adonis is a fun loving, free spirited equid. He isn't particularly bright, and is considered simple by most. This means that most insults and jokes go over his head. He also tends to be oblivious to equids hitting on him. Adonis is a loyal friend, and enjoys company from almost everyone. He forms very close bonds, and will stick by his friends to the end. He often tries to help others with their problems, but because he doesn't always understand why they're unhappy he really isn't much help. He can often be found bounding through the open mountain ranges or relaxing with his herd. Adonis is also a massive pig and is almost always eating something. His favourite foods are birch bark and surprisingly, dandelions. Flowers and all. He likes how the milk tastes. However, this does give him rather bad breath.
