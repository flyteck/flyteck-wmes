General

Overview

Romulus is fairly antisocial. He doesn't like to make close friends, and travels alone. That being said, he's definitely friendly. He loves meeting new equids, and he tends to get along well with everyone. He's super chill and very mellow, and nothing really bothers him. He'll listen to others' opinions, but nothing will change his mind on anything for the most part. Many equids have asked him to stay with their herds, but he always declines. He doesn't like staying in one place and likes to move at his own speed. He'll occasionally run into equids he knows, and when he does he'll hang around for a day or two before leaving, often at night without a single goodbye. It's not that he doesn't make friends, but he simply can't stand being tied down and has a preference, to some extent, for being alone. Although he's not really interested in a long term, settle-down relationship; he is open to partners as long as they understand that his visits will be temporary and non-exclusive.
