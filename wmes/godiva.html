General

Overview

Godiva is free spirited and outgoing. She's still young and sprightly, and doesn't plan on growing up any time soon. Godiva has a sharp tongue and a keen mind. While she knows how to show respect, she often chooses not to, and she's no pushover. She's very kind and willing to help when she sees fit, but if she doesn't like you or your cause then you're out of luck. She likes to weave random objects into her mane and tail, and can often be seen with silk ribbons tied around her tail. She's also usually chewing on a grass stem.
