General

Overview

Rocket is, first and foremost, a prankster. He loves nothing more than joking around and pulling pranks. He really rubs some people the wrong way, but he doesn't do it out of spite or hate. He's just playing around and, more or less, expects the same to be done to him. Rocket is also a fairly nomadic equid, loving to explore. He's not afraid of anything, and gets himself into some pretty tough spots because of it. He absolutely hates being as short as he is, but he doesn't let it hinder him. He's quite the spitfire, being both arrogant and stubborn. His pet peeve is being mocked for his height. He also doesn't like having to look up to others. When you really get to know him he's a great guy, but he's almost never totally serious. He forms very tight relationships and will stick up with his life for those he really cares about.
