Overview

Ladybird is definitely a little lady. She's dainty, with a sweet and shy attitude. Honolulu has worked with Ladybird to teach her the proper ways of conducting oneself, and although she thinks she has done a good job, Ladybird has never really been quiet and polite on the inside. That's just the side she introduces to others, but her closest friends know her true colours. Around Rocket in specific, Lady is a playful, risk-loving little filly. Sure, she enjoys walking through summer fields and sleeping in the sun, but she loves nothing more than racing through the mountains. She'd also give anything to be able to sass an older equid. Or anyone really. She's really just a little girl with big ideas.
