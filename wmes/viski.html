General

Overview

Viski is a very outgoing and friendly equid. She loves other equids, and always wants to be everyone's friend. She can come on a bit overenthusiastic, but she means well. She's quick to offend and quicker to forgive, which means she's very easy to take advantage of. However, she isn't stupid, and will only take abuse for so long. Her favourite pastimes mainly consist of simply hanging with friends, although she does like stargazing. She's a great equid to rely on and will always have her friend's back. She's terrible for advice though; her overly positive outlook and forgiving nature means she has trouble seeing flaws and bad people. So when people ask for help with a problem she tends to not understand the issue. She's very hard to upset (except if you offend her, but it last for about ten seconds then she's good), and is always in a sunny mood.
