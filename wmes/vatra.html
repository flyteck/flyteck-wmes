General

Overview

Vatra is a punk. She's tough, arrogant, and rather selfish. She's very ambitious, and always fighting to be the best. She's quick tempered and doesn't take any sass (or anything she dubs sass). She's very blunt and doesn't sugar coat things (most of her comments tend to be sarcastic and particularly caustic). People generally find her rude and abrasive, which she doesn't take well. She's quick to fight, and doesn't tend to think things over. She's also exceedingly stubborn; she won't change her mind, even if she knows she's wrong (for the sake of her pride). That being said, she's also very loyal. If you manage to get on her good side you'll have a very loyal (albeit overprotective) friend. She's really not fond of most other equids though; she finds most of them confrontational (hypocrite) and rude. When she does find someone she likes she can actually be rather clingy. Despite her loud attitude she's actually a little insecure.
