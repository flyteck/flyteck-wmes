General

Overview

Aleus is a proud, confident, and self-assured stallion. He has the knowledge and experience to back up his decisions, and it's not hard to see why he's a natural born leader. He's compassionate and kind, but not overly friendly. He tends to keep pleasantries to a minimum and is very businessy. However, that doesn't mean he's unemotional or cold; it's just that he rarely expressions emotions openly. He's very protective over his family and friends and will neutralize any threats he sees. He's in top physical condition, and is very strong as far as equids go. He's very intimidating and not to be messed with. While he prefers to handle arguments with grace and often gives the benefit of the doubt, if you take a step out of line you will sorely regret it.
