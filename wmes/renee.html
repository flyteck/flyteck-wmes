General

Overview

Renee is bitter and spiteful. She rarely talks to others, and dislikes company. Deep down she wants friends and to be accepted, but it's not like she'd ever tell anyone that. She doesn't place trust easily, and normally works hard to convince herself she's happy alone. She tends to be blunt and hurt others' feelings, although she always regrets it afterwords. She's very determined and unwavering; it's hard to convince her of anything. And even if you do, she's not going to act on it. Renee is loyal and very defensive of her friends, but it's nearly impossible to get anywhere near there with her. The only equids she befriends are those she respects and those too stubborn to leave her alone.
