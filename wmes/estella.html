General

Overview

At first glance, you'd expect Estella to be quiet and mellow. You'd be wrong. Along with rarely heading outside of her fenced in pen and being fully blind, Estella is small and dainty. But she's not the docile, quiet little mare you'd expect. She's spunky and playful, with a great sense of humour and a mischievous streak. Did your helmet go missing? It's probably in her stall. Horses got loose again? That would be her. She's quite the menace around the barn, but she means well. She might be blind, but her ears and nose work perfectly fine. She loves to play pranks and jokes just to hear peoples' reactions. Despite her best intentions, some people can find her irritating. Estella loves to make friends, and always enjoys the company of all other creatures, be it equids, people, or anything in between.
