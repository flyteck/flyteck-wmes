General

Overview

Classical is a sweet, innocent equid who anyone would love to be around. She can sometimes be a bit shy and introverted, although she doesn't mind quiet company. She's very compassionate and willing to help anyone who will let her. She's very bright, and takes an intellectual and perfectionistic approach to everything she does. Classical has a delicate, polite, nature that she mainly shows through careful planning and tolerance. Although she's very open and compassionate, Classical is not gullible or foolhardy. She is honestly a high-class intellectual, and will not be pushed around or fooled easily. She isn't afraid to put her hoof down if the situation calls for it, and she will always stick up for what she thinks is right, although she prefers to do this peacefully if she can. She rarely burdens herself with her own problems and never over-reacts when a tragedy has taken place - she is a master at self-control.
