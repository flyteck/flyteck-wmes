General

Overview

Zach is quite the flirt. He likes to "play the field" per say, although he does intend to settle down if he ever finds the right 'quid. But he has pretty high standards. Besides the fact that he flirts with any female equid (that isn't too young), he tends to be a bit harsh and overly sarcastic, but whenever he hurts someones feelings, he's quick to apologize. He has a rather good sense of humour, and never tends to be too serious. He can sometimes seem to have his head in the clouds, but he's really just thinking about more interesting things. Many older equids find him immature and foolish, but Zach just wants to have some fun in life. Once you get to know him well, he's a loyal friend who will stay by your side until the end. That being said, he's often alone and doesn't make many friends. Zach's rather hard to get close to and doesn't tend to understand others, as he has a different outlook on life compared to most. He's also fairly smart, although he can often be a bit slow on the uptake.
