General

Overview

Mocha, in one word, is derpy. She's not particularly bright, and is very easily distracted. She likes to run, jump, and play, and she tends to bother other equids by being overly hyper and rambunctious. She likes to pull on other equids' ears and tails, which nobody really enjoys. She can't be serious and is always trying to play. She acts much younger than she is. Mocha can also be rather skittish and antsy, normally when someone is trying to give her some form of responsibility. She doesn't like serious commitments or anything that involves much forethought and planning.
